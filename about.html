About Us

Welcome to Shri Ram Global School

Welcome to Shri Ram Global School, Ludhiana - where knowledge meets compassion, and young minds embark on a journey of holistic education. Established with a vision to nurture future leaders and compassionate individuals, Shri Ram Global School stands as a beacon of academic excellence and character development.

At Shri Ram Global School, we are committed to providing a stimulating learning environment that empowers students to excel academically, socially, and emotionally. Our mission is to foster a love for learning, instil a sense of integrity, and cultivate the skills necessary for a dynamic and ever-changing world.

Our Rich History
Founded in 2018, Shri Ram Global School has a storied history rooted in a commitment to providing quality education and shaping the leaders of tomorrow. From its humble beginnings to its current standing as a beacon of academic excellence, our school's journey reflects a legacy of passion, perseverance, and a dedication to nurturing young minds.

Throughout our history, Shri Ram Global School has achieved numerous milestones. Whether it be academic achievements, innovative programs, or the success stories of our alumni, each milestone is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our entire school community.

7c’s By the SRGS

7 C's Blueprint

By adhering to these principles, individuals can navigate communication challenges skillfully, fostering understanding and connection in various contexts.

Confidence

Students passing out of Shri Ram institutions are extremely confident and exhibit tremendous self-belief in themselves and their abilities to attain the impossible.

Collaboration

Students at Shri Ram Global School learn collaboration, i.e. working together to achieve a common goal, a necessary 21st Century requirement. It equips them with interpersonal skills, courtesy, patience, deeper understanding and leadership qualities.

Compassion

Compassion is the basis of all morality and students of Shri Ram Global School are compassionate which helps them remain good human beings. It is a virtue by which one shows emotional capacities of empathy and sympathy for the suffering of fellow human beings.

Commitment

Commitment is an interaction associated with obligation or pledges.Students of Shri Ram Global School are committed to any job or activities they undertake in life.

Curiosity

Shri Ram Global School students are invited with a position to, Theirquality is developed by encouraging them to ask questions to satisfy their inquisitiveness, instead of being passive learners.

Critical Thinking

Critical thinking is a composite facility of the intellect that reasons, questions, conceptualises, analyses and applies. Every student at SRGS is encouraged to develop critical thinking in his or her own formative years.

Creativity

Shri Ram Global School students are immensely creative in their approach to any problem. Creativity is the process of bringing something new into being.Creativity requires passion which is found in abundance among the students of Shri Ram Global School.
